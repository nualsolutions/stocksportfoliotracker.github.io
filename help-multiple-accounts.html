I can't use Portfolio Tracker Pro (I have two accounts)

You can't purchase Portfolio tracker subscription due to the next error?


" Error: The device account already owns this product."

This message means that the Google Play/Apple ID account linked to your smartphone has already purchased Portfolio tracker Pro from the smartphone account logged in.
To solve this, make sure your app is logged into the account you want to use to purchase the subscription (you can check the account you are logged into from the "Preferences>account" section). And make sure that the store Google Play/Apple ID account in use does note have a subscription.
If you're logged into another account, first completely log out (Preferences > Log out), and then log into the account you want to use.
